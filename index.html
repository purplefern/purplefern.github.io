Effective Altruism

I identify with the Effective Altruism movement.

For those who are unfamiliar, EA is

...the project of (1) using evidence and reason to find the most promising causes to work on, and (2) taking action, by using our time and money to do the most good we can.

I stay involved with EA since it generates research and discussions that interest me.

I believe that EA helps with or will likely help with improving contemporaneous well-being for humanity, improving outcomes for posterity, maintaining civilizational stability, and reducing collective sentient suffering.


Forecasting

I have been forecasting on the website Metaculus since June 2021.

I find estimating future outcomes entertaining.

I've enjoyed interacting with the forecasting community, and they've helped me to improve my forecasting.

My highest rank was/is 250 out ~10000 forecasters on Metaculus as of December 18th, 2021.

I believe my Metaculus rank is a weak signal for my forecasting accuracy.

The overwhelming majority of my forecasts are subjective estimates.

Many questions on Metaculus have point ranges that fail to measure forecasting skill adequately.

Many of my forecasts have been on such questions.
